Book Description
-----------------

The ONLY official guide to the 2015 release of the #1 bestselling personal finance software“Intuit-approved and loaded with secrets, this book provides you with recipes for financial success.” –Scott Cook, Co-Founder, Intuit, Inc.
Quicken 2015: The Official Guide shows you how to get the most out of the this powerful software’s new features and mange finances at home and on mobile devices. Step-by-step instructions and illustrations demonstrate how to set up and balance personal banking and credit accounts, pay bills, monitor balances online, and track investments.
This Intuit-authorized book offers professional advice to help you reduce debt, stay on budget, save money, and plan for a secure financial future. “In My Experience” sidebars highlight key benefits of Quicken’s features.
